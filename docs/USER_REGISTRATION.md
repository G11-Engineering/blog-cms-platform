# User Registration Guide

## How to Create Your Account

Welcome to the WSO2 Blog Platform! This guide will walk you through the account creation process.

### Registration Process

1. **Visit the Platform**
   - Navigate to [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) (or your platform URL)

2. **Click "Sign Up"**
   - Find the **"Sign Up"** button in the top navigation bar
   - You'll be redirected to an information page

3. **Read the Registration Instructions**
   - The page explains what will happen next
   - Auto-redirect countdown begins (10 seconds)
   - You can click "Continue to Registration Now" to skip the countdown

4. **Complete Asgardeo Registration**
   - You'll be redirected to WSO2 Asgardeo's secure registration page
   - Fill in the required information:
     - **Email Address** - Your valid email
     - **Password** - Choose a strong password
     - **First Name** - Your first name
     - **Last Name** - Your last name
   - Accept the terms and conditions
   - Click **"Register"**

5. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Look for a verification email from Asgardeo
   - Click the verification link in the email
   - Your account will be activated

6. **Return and Login**
   - Go back to the blog platform
   - Click **"Login"** in the navigation bar
   - You'll be redirected to Asgardeo SSO
   - Enter your credentials
   - You'll be logged into the platform

7. **Start Creating Content**
   - You're now registered as an **Author**!
   - Click **"Write Post"** to create your first blog post

## Troubleshooting

### I Didn't Receive the Verification Email

**Solution:**
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Wait 5-10 minutes (delivery can be delayed)
3. Return to Asgardeo registration page and request a new verification email
4. If issues persist, contact platform support

### My Registration Link Expired

**Solution:**
- Verification links expire after 24 hours
- Return to the registration page and start the process again
- Or request a new verification email from Asgardeo

### I Can't Login After Registering

**Possible Causes:**
1. **Email not verified** - Complete email verification first
2. **Wrong credentials** - Check email and password
3. **Account not activated** - Wait a few minutes after email verification

**Solution:**
- Ensure you've clicked the email verification link
- Try resetting your password via Asgardeo
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Contact support if issues continue

### I'm Seeing "Token Missing Email Claim" Error

**Solution:**
This error has been fixed in the latest version. If you see it:
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Log out completely
3. Try logging in again
4. Contact an administrator if the error persists

### I Need Higher Permissions (Editor or Admin Role)

**Solution:**
- Author is the default role for all new users
- To request Editor or Admin permissions:
  1. Contact a platform administrator
  2. Explain why you need elevated permissions
  3. Admin will update your role in Asgardeo groups

**Admin Contact:**
- Email: admin@cms.com (default admin)
- Or use the platform's contact form

### I Want to Change My Email or Password

**Email Change:**
- Log into Asgardeo Console directly
- Update your profile information
- Verify the new email address
- Changes sync automatically on next login

**Password Change:**
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Follow Asgardeo's password reset process
- Or manage from Asgardeo Console ‚Üí Profile ‚Üí Security

### The Registration Page Keeps Redirecting

**Solution:**
- This is normal behavior - you're being sent to Asgardeo
- If stuck in a redirect loop:
  1. Clear browser cookies
  2. Open an incognito/private window
  3. Try the registration process again

### I Accidentally Created Multiple Accounts

**Solution:**
- Contact an administrator
- Provide both email addresses
- Admin can merge or delete duplicate accounts
- Only one account per user is allowed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is registration free?
Yes! Registration is completely free.

### Can I delete my account?
Yes, contact an administrator to request account deletion.

### Can I have multiple accounts?
No, one account per user. Multiple accounts may be removed.

### How long does verification take?
Verification emails are usually instant, but can take up to 10 minutes.

### Can I change my username?
Your username is based on your email prefix. Contact admin for changes.

### Do I need to register to read posts?
No, published posts are public. Registration is only needed to create content.

### What happens if I forget my password?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it via Asgardeo.

### Can I switch between roles?
No, role changes must be done by an administrator.
